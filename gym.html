Exercise Library 🏋️‍♂️

Chest Exercises

Bench Press (Compound): A multi-joint exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Tip: Ensure your feet stay flat on the floor and lower the bar slowly.

Chest Fly (Isolation): Focuses on isolating the chest muscles with minimal shoulder involvement.

Tip: Maintain a slight bend in your elbows throughout the movement.

Push-up (Compound): A classic bodyweight exercise that engages the chest, triceps, and shoulders.

Tip: Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.

Back Exercises

Deadlift (Compound): A full-body exercise that targets the back, legs, and core.

Tip: Keep your back straight and hips aligned with your shoulders.

Lat Pulldown (Isolation): Focuses on isolating the lats and upper back.

Tip: Don’t lean back too much; keep your torso stable.

Pull-up (Compound): A challenging bodyweight exercise that targets the upper back and arms.

Tip: Ensure full range of motion by pulling your chin over the bar.

Leg Exercises

Squat (Compound): A fundamental exercise that targets the quads, glutes, and hamstrings.

Tip: Keep your knees behind your toes and maintain a neutral spine.

Leg Extension (Isolation): Focuses on isolating the quadriceps.

Tip: Don’t lock your knees at the top of the movement.

Lunges (Compound): A great unilateral exercise for the quads and glutes.

Tip: Keep your torso upright and ensure your knee doesn’t pass your toes.

Shoulder Exercises

Overhead Press (Compound): A shoulder-dominant exercise that also engages the triceps.

Tip: Keep your core tight and avoid arching your lower back.

Lateral Raise (Isolation): Focuses on the lateral deltoids for shoulder width.

Tip: Don’t swing the weights; use controlled movements.

Arnold Press (Compound): A variation of the overhead press that targets all three shoulder heads.

Tip: Rotate the palms inward at the bottom for a full range of motion.

Arm Exercises

Chin-up (Compound): A bodyweight exercise that targets both the biceps and back.

Tip: Focus on pulling with your arms rather than your back.

Bicep Curl (Isolation): Targets the biceps with minimal assistance from other muscles.

Tip: Keep your elbows stationary and avoid swinging the weights.

Tricep Dip (Isolation): Focuses solely on the triceps.

Tip: Keep your shoulders away from your ears and elbows close to your body.

Core Exercises

Plank (Compound): An isometric exercise that targets the core and engages multiple muscles.

Tip: Maintain a straight line from head to heels and avoid letting your hips drop.

Crunch (Isolation): Focuses on the abdominal muscles.

Tip: Don’t pull on your neck; keep your hands at your sides.

Leg Raise (Compound): A core exercise that targets the lower abs.

Tip: Engage your core as you lift your legs and avoid swinging them.
